Ben Nix-Bradley

BIO

  ... is a versatile artist and composer from Los Angeles who skillfully merges digital art, software development, and formal music composition skills into creative collaborations. He has earned several prestigious scholarships from the Los Angeles Community College District and California State University Northridge, including the Herb Alpert Music Center, PAMA Mark Brunner and Jerry & Carol Goldsmith Awards.

  In 2021, he graduated with top honors from the Herb Alpert Music Center at Los Angeles City College under the study of Dr. Kevin Kelly. In 2023, he earned a Bachelor of Music from the CSUN Media and Commercial Writing program under Elizabeth Sellers. Ben shares his expertise in music production and technology individually and as a supervisor at the University Library's Creative Maker Studio.

  Proficient in linear and interactive storytelling pipelines, Ben focuses on composing music for creative projects while expanding his career in media production and education. He has plans for graduate studies in music and technology.
