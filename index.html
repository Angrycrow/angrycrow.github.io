BIO

Welcome to my site! I am a composer and digital artist from Los Angeles, California . These days I'm studying media composition at Cal-State Northridge. I graduated from Los Angeles City College's music academy as a Herb Alpert Scholar in 2021. I've been working on this webpage for fun and to teach myself new things about software development. Often times personal websites are about as interesting as business cards so I want to make something here that is at least a little more interesting as I go. Come back sometime and try to spot the surprises I'm planning to hide among these words.
